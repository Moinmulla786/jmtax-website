Record pay runs here — never as bank transactions.
Enter gross pay and the CPP / EI / tax you withheld — get the exact figures from CRA's Payroll Deductions Online Calculator (PDOC) ↗ (always current rates, maintained by CRA). Each pay run posts wages expense and two liabilities: Wages Payable (net pay owed to the employee) and Payroll Deductions Payable (owed to CRA).
Then, when the money leaves the bank: categorize the net-pay withdrawal as Wages Payable and the CRA remittance as Payroll Deductions Payable — the liabilities clear and nothing is ever double-counted. The controls banner flags any mismatch.
