Tools to Help You Stay on Top of Your Finances

Free tools and templates to make bookkeeping and tax season easier. Use them yourself — and reach out whenever you'd like our team to take it from here.

Ledger — Bookkeeping App

A complete Canadian small-business bookkeeping tool: record transactions and generate your P&L, Balance Sheet, Trial Balance, HST summary and GIFI (T2) codes — then export to Excel or PDF.

Corporate Bookkeeping Template

A ready-to-use spreadsheet to track your corporate income and expenses through the year and keep your books organized for filing.

Personal Tax Checklist

Everything you need to gather before filing your personal taxes, so nothing gets missed and your return goes smoothly.

A note on the Ledger app: your data is saved only in your own web browser on your own device — nothing is uploaded to us or stored on a server. That keeps your information private, but it also means it won't sync between devices, and clearing your browser data will erase it. Export to Excel or PDF regularly to keep your own copies.
